We have redesigned the diagnostic journey for the digital age. No more waiting in long queues for physical reports.

Through our proprietary patient portal, you can track the status of your sample in real-time. Once the admin approves the results, an automated email is triggered, allowing you to access your encrypted PDF reports from anywhere in the world.
